Robotics Researcher

Harbin Institute of Technology

State Key Laboratory of Robotics and System

I am a doctor candidate of mechanical engineering supervised by Jie Zhao at the state key laboratory of robotics and system in Harbin Institute of Technology. Meanwhile, I am a research intern supervised by Sylvain Calinon in Idiap Research Institute. My research interests include robot skill learning, learning from demonstration and imitation learning. I am also interested in optimal control and reinforcement learning.
